|ABOUT KLEO|

The Kleo Hotel, Zirakpur, Chandigarh is the place where elegance meets comfort at an affordable price with a royal treatment to those out on vacation, official or family trip. Nestled in the heartland of the Chandigarh Tricity comprising of Chandigarh, Panchkula, Mohali & Zirakpur besides other neighbouring areas including the Morni Hills in Haryana, Kasauli, Chail & Shimla in Himachal Pradesh, princely town of Patiala & Bhakra Nangal Dam at Nangal in Punjab.
